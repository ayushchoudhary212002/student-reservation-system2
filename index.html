About the event

Techracy is the annual technical festival of Central Institute of Technology Kokrajhar which is organized every year. The festival promises to be a perfect fusion of innovative ideas and enthusiasm. Extending one's limit out of boundary one can express themselves. Starting from competiton to workshops it includes many more events. Youth get a chance to motivate themselves through lecture series by high voltage appearences across India. Discover yourself through literary events and with worldclass photoghrapy in photography challenge. Bring out your concept in the form of hilarious models in model display competition like Jouga and Alayaron etc.

About CIT Central Institute of Technology (CIT), Kokrajhar is situated in Kokrajhar District of Bodoland Territorial Council (BTC) in Assam. CIT has been established for the basic objective of fulfilling the aspirations of the Bodo People relating to their cultural identity, language, education and overall economic development of the region and to impart Bodo youths with requisite technological and vocational training to produce the required manpower to give the impetus to economic growth of this area and to integrate the Bodo People into the mainstream of Technical and Vocational Education. It is a Centrally Funded Institute under the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India.
